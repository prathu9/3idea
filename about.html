ABOUT US

WHAT ARE WE?

3idea Technology LLP is a high-tech product supplier which includes 3D printers, 3D Scanners, 3D Pens, Filaments and Resin. We've sold more than five thousand products in over a few years. We are also on prominent e-commerce websites such as Amazon, Flipkart and many more.

OUR WORK

We sell high-quality 3D printers and other products with excellent services, online and offline. 3idea has a section for robotics and lab setups for technical organisations and educational institutions. 3idea has a rigorous supply of products, which helped us to serve thousands of customers in 6-7 years

ACHIEVEMENTS AND ACCOMPLISHMENTS

We have aided thousands of customers with their 3D printing needs. We are authorized resellers of Zortrax Desktop LPD products. We also participated in the 3D PRINT EXPO-2019. We are certified as resellers of Creality and Ender also. 3idea is also a certified distributor of Snapmaker. We also come under the top 10 thousand selling partner club of Amazon. We have achieved huge milestones and awards for various activities in the 3D Printing industry. We are also certified by IndiaMart for meeting the requirements of their TrustSEAL, as we got more than 5 thousand customers in Feb 2022

WHY CHOOSE 3IDEA?

3idea promotes inexpensive and trustworthy products. Our products are of premium quality and are very sturdy for any user. We have a vast range of items, from 3D printers to Laser engravers. We also have various resins and filaments for printers. We are proud to say that all our products are vigorous and the best in the market.
